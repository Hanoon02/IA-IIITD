About Our Institute

Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology, Delhi (aka. IIIT-Delhi or IIIT-D) was created as a State University by an act of Govt. of NCT of Delhi (The IIIT Delhi Act, 2007) empowering it to do research and development, and grant degrees.

In a relatively short time, it has earned an excellent reputation in India and abroad for being a center of quality education and research in IT and interdisciplinary areas.

IIIT Delhi was established

IIIT Delhi was established by the Government of Delhi. The institute was officially inaugurated on July 10, 2008.

The first academic session

The first academic session commenced with B.Tech programs in Computer Science and Electronics & Communication Engineering.

Permanent Campus

IIIT Delhi moved to its permanent campus located in Okhla Phase-III, New Delhi

Introduced Postgraduate Programs

The institute introduced postgraduate programs: M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), M.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), and Ph.D. programs.

10th Foundation Day

IIIT Delhi celebrated its 10th foundation day.

Institute of National Importance

IIIT Delhi was recognized as an 'Institute of National Importance' by the Government of India.

Reached New Milestone

IIIT Delhi further expanded its programs and research initiatives, establishing itself as a leading institute in the field of information technology and computer science. IIIT Delhi has been continuously evolving, enhancing its academic programs, research output, and infrastructure to cater to the growing demands of the IT industry and academic excellence in India.

15th Foundation Day

The institute celebrated its 15th foundation day and continues to expand its academic offerings and research activities.
